Tesla Note

Action

2021


In Tesla Note, a group of spies known as the Tesla Note team are tasked with preventing the world from falling into chaos by recovering the fragments of a mysterious device known as the Tesla Cube. The team, consisting of skilled individuals with unique abilities, must navigate dangerous missions and face powerful adversaries in their quest to save the world from destruction. With the fate of humanity hanging in the balance, the team must race against time to uncover the secrets of the Tesla Cube.
