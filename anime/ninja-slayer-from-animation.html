Ninja Slayer From Animation

Action

2015



In a city overrun by rival ninja clans, a man seeking revenge becomes possessed by a powerful ninja spirit, becoming Ninja Slayer. Fueled by a thirst for vengeance, he embarks on a mission to rid the city of evil ninjas, battling his way through the criminal underworld with his deadly ninja skills. With the help of fellow ninja warriors, Ninja Slayer faces off against formidable foes in epic battles where only the strongest will survive.
