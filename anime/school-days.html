School Days

Romance

2007


Makoto Itou is an ordinary high school student who becomes entangled in a complex love triangle with two girls, Sekai Saionji and Kotonoha Katsura. As his feelings grow for both girls, Makoto's indecisiveness leads to a series of uncontrollable events that spiral out of control. The relationships become strained, secrets are revealed, and jealousy ensues in this dramatic and unpredictable high school romance.
