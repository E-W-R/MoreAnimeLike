Sol Levante

Action

2020



In Sol Levante, a group of traditional Japanese artisans must come together to create stunning hand-painted animations using cutting-edge technology. As they grapple with the fusion of old and new techniques, they strive to push the boundaries of their craft and bring their unique vision to life. Through dedication and creativity, they seek to honor the art of animation and create a masterpiece that will captivate audiences around the world.
