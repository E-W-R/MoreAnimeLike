Garzey's Wing

Isekai

1996


Garzey's Wing follows a high school student who gets mysteriously transported to a parallel world on the brink of war. With the help of a mystical being and a powerful artifact known as the Garzey's Wing, he must navigate political intrigue and battle against formidable foes to find a way back home. As he struggles to understand his new role in this foreign land, he discovers hidden truths about himself and his own destiny.
