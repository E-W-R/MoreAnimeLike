Kowabon

Other Genre

2015


Kowabon follows different characters as they experience terrifying and supernatural events through their smartphones and social media. Each episode delves into a new horror story, exploring the dark and chilling consequences of modern technology. Viewers are taken on a haunting journey as the characters navigate through the eerie and unsettling mysteries that unfold from their digital interactions.
