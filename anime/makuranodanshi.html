makuranodanshi

Other Genre

2015



Makura no Danshi follows a unique concept where each episode features a different pillow boy that speaks directly to the viewer, providing comfort as they drift off to sleep. From a kind senpai to a mysterious prince, each boy offers a different fantasy scenario to help ease the viewer into slumber. With soothing voices and heartwarming stories, this anime invites viewers to experience a moment of relaxation and tranquility before bedtime.
