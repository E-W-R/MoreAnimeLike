Calamity of the Zombie Girl

Action

2018


After a forbidden experiment goes awry, a group of high school students accidentally awaken two dormant zombies. The revived girls possess superhuman abilities, and the students must now confront the consequences of their actions as a secret organization sets out to capture the zombies. Amidst chaos and danger, the students must decide whether to befriend or betray the zombie girls as they uncover the truth behind their resurrection.
