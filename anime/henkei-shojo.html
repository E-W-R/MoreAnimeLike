Henkei Shojo

Other Genre

2017


Henkei Shojo follows the story of high school student Ichigo, who discovers she has the ability to transform into a powerful battle robot. Alongside her friends, each with their own unique transformation abilities, they must come together to defend the city from mysterious invading monsters. As they navigate the challenges of their dual lives, Ichigo and her friends uncover secrets about the origins of their powers and the true nature of their enemies.
