Cipher

Drama

1989


In a world where everyone possesses a unique power called a Cipher, a young boy named Hiro discovers he is the only one without this ability. Determined to find his place in society, Hiro befriends a group of Cipher users and sets out on a journey to uncover the truth behind his mysterious past. As he delves deeper into the secrets of Ciphers, Hiro learns that he may hold the key to unlocking a power greater than anyone could imagine.
