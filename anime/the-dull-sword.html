The Dull Sword

Comedy

1917



In a world where magical swords are the key to power, a young blacksmith discovers a seemingly ordinary and dull sword. Little does he know that this sword holds a great secret and has the power to change the course of history. With enemies seeking to claim the sword for themselves, the blacksmith must now embark on a dangerous journey to uncover the sword's true potential and protect it from falling into the wrong hands.
