GYO: Tokyo Fish Attack!

Mystery

2011



In GYO: Tokyo Fish Attack!, a mysterious, foul-smelling fog engulfs Tokyo, bringing with it deadly fish with mechanical legs. As chaos erupts and the city is overrun with these monstrous creatures, Kaori and her friends must navigate the terrifying new reality to uncover the origins of the invasion and find a way to survive. With danger lurking at every turn, they must confront their fears and fight for survival against the bizarre and deadly threat.
