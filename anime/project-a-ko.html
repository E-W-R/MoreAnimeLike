Project A-Ko

Action

1986


Project A-Ko follows the lives of two high school girls, A-Ko and B-Ko, who find themselves in constant rivalry over a boy named C-Ko. As they navigate through their everyday school lives, they soon discover hidden secrets about their past and their true identities. The girls must confront these revelations while dealing with giant robots, aliens, and a mysterious flying object that threatens their town. The film is a mix of comedy, action, and sci-fi elements.
