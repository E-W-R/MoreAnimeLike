Gakuen Handsome

Comedy

2016


Gakuen Handsome is a parody anime that follows the hilarious adventures of a transfer student named Yoshiki Maeda at the all-boys Baramon High School. Despite his ordinary appearance, Yoshiki quickly becomes entangled with a group of eccentric and overly dramatic schoolmates, including the school's handsome yet bizarrely quirky student council. As the series unfolds, viewers are taken on a wild ride of absurdity and comedic mishaps as Yoshiki navigates the unpredictable and over-the-top world of Baramon High School.
