Midori

Drama

1992



Midori follows the story of a young girl living in a circus who befriends a dwarf magician. When tragedy strikes, Midori discovers a dark and twisted world behind the circus's cheerful facade. As she navigates the brutal reality surrounding her, she must find the strength to survive and protect those she cares about. With themes of resilience and friendship, Midori explores the power of hope in the face of despair.
